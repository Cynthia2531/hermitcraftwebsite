Hermitcraft

What is Hermitcraft?

Hermitcraft is a whitelist only minecraft server which started in April
2012 by Generikb which is currently administered by Xisumavoid.
They have had several modded and vanilla series, currently in the 9th
vanilla season and 11th year of running. It's members, called Hermits
are popular minecraft creators who have been recognized by other
Hermits and invited to join Hermitcraft.

Who are the players on Hermitcraft?

The Hermits are what the fandom refers to the players on the server.
There is currently 25 whitelisted Hermits (23 active, 2 inactive) who often post youtube videos on the server or stream the server on twitch.
